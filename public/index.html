Our Mission

Our mission is to make online safety easy to understand. We share simple steps you can use immediately to avoid scams, protect privacy, and feel more confident online.

Common Online Scams

Phishing Emails

Fake emails that try to steal passwords or personal information.

Tech Support Scams

Someone pretends to be support and asks for money or remote access.

Fake Shopping Sites

Sites that look real but steal payment info or never deliver products.

Social Media Fraud

Fake profiles and DMs designed to trick you or steal information.
